Hello Kigali Website

Kigali city discovery web app

This app was built by local street teams venturing out to find the best spots and their exact locations. It could be the most trusted app for where places in Rwanda are exactly located.

Currently, Hello Kigali has over 1,000 sites like restaurants, cafes, nightclubs, hotels, embassy, shopping, medical, tourism, wifi locations, and other desired locations. The 2020 version has ratings and other social features.

Hello Kigali is a free city discovery guide for restaurants, cafes, WiFi, and more. Explore, collect badges, and rate your favorite sites as you pick what is 'Best of Kigali.' The WiFi indicator is perfect for anyone new to Kigali, Rwanda, and seeking the best restaurants and cafes to visit and get some work done.
